A lightweight system to track Contact Form 7 Events in Google Analytics. 

== Description ==
Track form submissions that are successful, invalid, failed, spam or just just track everything.

Contact Form 7 forms are tracked by their names in Google Analytics under the Events tracking section.

Tracks all Contact Form 7 forms on your website automatically.

== Installation ==
Download from WordPress as any other plugin, install and activate.

Update your Tracking ID in the settings and choose your options.
